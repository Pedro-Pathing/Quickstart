/// Driving
Speed starts at 0.8
Switches from 0.8 -> 1.0 and from 1.0 -> 0.8
Press Touchpad to change speed

Use joysticks for movement

/// Shooting
Use right bumper to turn the shooter on
Dpad up increases speed by 0.1
Dpad down decreases speed by 0.1

/// Intake
use left bumper to turn intake on
Dpad right increases speed by 0.1
Dpad down decreases speed by 0.1
